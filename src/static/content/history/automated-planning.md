During a research internship at [MuLab](https://mulab.ai), I worked on model acquisition in automated planning—inferring action models from observed plan traces.

That work included [MACQ](/#project-macq), a Python toolkit and foundational API for model-acquisition research, and [Bauhaus](/#project-bauhaus), an open-source library for constructing propositional-logic encodings. I designed and authored MACQ and continue to maintain it alongside Christian Muise.
